Cover Letter

- My Application

I started out my sales career in ecommerce before moving to a hardware support focused role. Currently, I am the sole relationship owner in Ireland for the sale of non-IBM support. I work with large Commercial & Government organisations helping them to improve their IT infrastructure by reducing complexity, decreasing costs and ensuring availability of systems.

This means developing and implementing tailored integrated IT multivendor service solutions to increase the value obtained from data centre environments. As my role & responsibilities have increased, so too has my requirement to become more knowledgeable in the field of software as this is becoming an increasingly important topic within the industry.

Funnily enough, this knowledge gap has inspired something within me that I’ve had since childhood. As a kid, I discovered the power of creating websites by way of HTML using Notepad. I thought this was magic. Then along came website scripts and ‘Flash’ scripting – what are others doing but more importantly, how are they doing it and how can I improve on it.

Somewhere along the line, I had to “concentrate on schoolwork”, stopped improving and ultimately forgot about the joy of (simple) programming. In 2020, as part of a promotion effort, I came up with the idea & created a simple to help save time & upskill newcomers and just like that, my love for programming was reawakened.

I have many ideas but have never been able to implement them due to a lack of skill. This course will enable me to put those ideas into reality and also be a massive factor when it comes to my credibility in my current career path. I do not know if I will make the full switch to a web or software developer upon completion of this course, but it is something I am seriously contemplating and look to seeing which way I want to go with it / it wants to bring me.
